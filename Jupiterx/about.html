Jupiter X Trading and Contracting is a dynamic and rapidly growing company based in Qatar, dedicated to excellence in Fire protection installation, maintenance and trading, MEP Works, Civil Construction, Fire Fighting & Fire Alarm Systems, Fire Equipment Trading and General contracting.
With a strong emphasis on quality, safety, and innovation, we provide comprehensive turnkey solutions tailored to the diverse needs of clients in the commercial, industrial, and residential sectors.

Fire Systems & Equipment Trading

Fire Fighting & Fire Alarm Systems and Fire Equipment Trading involve the supply, installation, and maintenance of essential fire safety solutions, including alarm systems, sprinklers, hydrants, and extinguishers.

MEP Works

These services ensure proper functioning of HVAC, power distribution, lighting, water supply, drainage, and fire safety systems, essential for safe and efficient building operations.

Civil Works

Civil Works involve the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure such as buildings, roads, bridges, drainage systems, and foundations. These projects form the backbone of physical development and ensure structural stability and long-term durability.

General Contracting

General Contracting involves managing and executing construction projects from start to finish. It includes coordinating labor, materials, equipment, and subcontractors to ensure timely, safe, and high-quality project delivery.
